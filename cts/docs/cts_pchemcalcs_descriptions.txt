External links to each of the physicochemical calculators are given below to provide additional information for the individual calculators.<br><br>

<a href="http://www2.epa.gov/tsca-screening-tools/epi-suitetm-estimation-program-interface" target="_blank" class="fadeMenu">EPI (Estimation Programs Interface) Suite</a> - EPI Suite is a Windows-based suite of physical/chemical property and environmental fate estimation programs developed by EPA and SRC, formerly Syracuse Research Corporation.<br><br>
